1. Threat Landscape (OSINT Intelligence)

Key attack vectors targeting financial institutions based on the latest global threat research.

  • Deepfakes & KYC Bypasses (e.g., GoldPickaxe) Harvesting biometric data and bypassing liveness checks via virtual camera injections and face-swap attacks.
  • Advanced Malware (Snowblind, Brokewell) Next-generation Trojans abusing seccomp and accessibility features to blind anti-tampering restrictions and read screens.
  • AI SIM Swapping & Account Takeover (ATO) Highly sophisticated attacks utilizing AI voice agents for automated telecom social engineering and instant identity takeover.

2. Talsec Detection Ecosystem

Millisecond-level compromise identification utilizing AI and multi-layered architecture.

  • Device Risk State API Instant, real-time queries for current risk scores and device status, accessible directly by the banking backend during critical flows.
  • Global Threat Intelligence Dashboard Collection and evaluation of billions of signals worldwide, providing real-time visibility into active threats and malware campaigns.
  • AppiCrypt® & RASP+ Telemetry Zero-trust cryptographic proof of integrity that instantly identifies and blocks compromised sessions, screen overlays, and emulators.

3. SOC/IR Kinetic Response Playbook

Immediate kinetic actions for the SOC and Fraud teams upon intercepting a high-risk score.

PHASE 1 Triage & Containment
  • API Drop: The backend instantly drops requests returning poor evaluations from the Device Risk State API.
  • App Auto-Kill: Talsec automatically terminates the app upon detecting security bypasses (e.g., seccomp abuse).
PHASE 2 Investigation
  • Threat Intelligence: Leverage the Talsec Portal for instant correlation and visualization of malware campaigns on a global scale.
  • Vector Analysis: Determine if the anomaly stems from an isolated virtual camera attack or a widespread ATO SIM swap campaign.
PHASE 3 Remediation
  • Session Revocation: Immediate invalidation of tokens and active sessions for the compromised Device ID.
  • SIM Swap Prevention: Secure future sessions with a strict cryptographic bond to the physical hardware (Device Binding).

4. Proactive Defense & Real-time Integration

Continuous integration of Talsec AI into business workflows for seamless OWASP MASVS compliance.

KYC & Transactional Anti-Fraud

Instant blocking of active threats. Before a large money transfer or identity verification (KYC), the backend queries the Device Risk State API. If a Virtual Camera or active screen recording is detected, the transaction is frozen immediately.

Network Hardening & Secrets Vault

Deployment of Dynamic TLS Pinning to eliminate Man-in-the-Middle (MiTM) attacks. Utilization of the Secrets Vault component for obfuscation and encryption of sensitive strings (Data at Rest).
