Incident Response and Monitoring

We maintain a formalized incident response plan to ensure a swift, effective, and coordinated response to any security event. This includes continuous monitoring to detect threats and a structured process to manage incidents from discovery to resolution.

Our Incident Response Process

1

Detect & Report

All employees report events to a central point. Urgent issues are escalated by phone.

2

Assess & Classify

Events are assessed and categorized as "Events" or critical "Incidents" based on impact.

3

Respond & Resolve

Action is taken based on defined response times, with urgent incidents addressed within 1 hour.

4

Analyze & Learn

A post-incident analysis identifies the root cause and lessons learned to prevent recurrence.

5

Monitor

Continuous monitoring of logs from cloud providers helps detect threats and validate fixes.
